Design a product with 16 hours of work time at a weekend startup hackathon.

Problem Space

How might we improve a parent’s connection with their child while they are in someone else’s care?

My Role

UX Designer and Project Manager.

Bragging Rights

First place winner in the competition!

Discovering the Problem

We conducted user interviews, competitor analysis, and market research to identify user needs and problems.

Insights

  • Discovered parents, especially first time parents, experience stress when leaving their child in someone else’s care. Also, they feel guilty because they may miss milestones in their children’s growth.
  • Parents want to make sure their children are cared for and developing.

Define our strategy and solution

We decided to focus on a solution for parents and caregiver to collaborate on a child’s care and development.

Features and ideas:

  • Enable multiple caregivers to collaborate on a child's care.
  • Feed with notes, photos, and videos.
  • Child's profile with important information.
  • Child's development plan.

Developed low fidelity prototype

Tested prototype and received feedback from participants

  • Received positive feedback on child's profile because it allows them to share important info with caregiver.
  • Parents want to see schedule and report of meals and naps.
  • Parents want to receive notifications in case of emergencies.

Developed high fidelity prototype

Created clickable prototype in InVision for demo.
