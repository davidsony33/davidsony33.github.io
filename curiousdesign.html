I'm curious about curious design in the world.

Some are good or interesting design. Some are design failures.

Savioke is a robot that delivers small items to hotel guests. The designers decided not to make it human like so hotel staff wouldn't fear it taking their jobs. They designed it to look more like an appliance.

Google promotes their wifi service with interactive advertising. Users get a gift by answering all four trivia questions correctly. The reward and trivia creates a positive experience for the user.

Why would you add a step for people to trip on?!

Did they conduct needs analysis and user interviews to develop this service? I'd be surprise if they get more than two customers a day.

Radio face blocks the display so the user can't see the radio station.
