About Me

Hello! I'm a UX Designer and a graduate student in Human Computer Interaction + Design at UC Irvine.

I'm interested in making products more enjoyable and useful. In particular, I'm interested in designing enterprise software to help people work more efficiently and effectively.

I believe in an iterative process that begins with understanding the user and the problem. I love the problem solving nature of design.

I have two dogs - both pug mixes. I enjoy running new trails around the Bay Area.
