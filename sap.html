SAP

Designing the hospital of the future.

My Role

User Experience Designer, Scrum Master

Discovering the Problems

Doctors and nurses are burdened with documentation.

Conducting field research and interviews gave me insight into doctor's and nurse's workflows, pain points, and challenges. A team of nurses at a children's hospital used their personal cell phones and paper as work arounds to process sensitive work orders. Also, documentation in emergency rooms pose additional challenges due to the priority of cases. A trauma team member used printed templates to take notes as the rest of the team shouted information while stabalizing the patient.

Key Findings

Doctors and nurses spend more than 50% of their time using eletronic health records.

Nurses use paper and pen to keep track of patient information.

Hospital use a number of systems that are not interoperable creating work inefficiencies.

Developing the Strategy

We discovered plenty of problems to tackle. I created storyboards to help illustrate the challenges users face. This helped us develop solutions we wanted to pursue and determine our strategy.
